# 💣 MINEPATH

> *One chicken. One minefield. No second chances.*

MINEPATH is a browser-based minesweeper survival game. Navigate a hidden minefield tile by tile, find the checkpoint, and get out before the timer runs out — or before you step on something that ends your run instantly.

No hearts. No retries. One wrong tap and you're done.

## How to Play

- Tap tiles to reveal them
- Avoid mines hidden across the grid
- Reach the **checkpoint tile** in the corner to clear the level
- Collect **🌾 seeds** by surviving — seeds are your currency
- Use **power-ups** hidden in safe tiles — shields, slow-mo, reveals, double score, magnet
- Timer drains faster as levels increase — not just shorter, actually faster

Step on a mine → instant game over. No hearts. No cushion.

## What's New in V4 — The Sanctuary Update

The biggest update yet. MinePath now has a living world, a skill system, an endless mode, and deep progression that keeps going long after the levels end.

### 🏡 The Sanctuary
The home screen is now your **Sanctuary** — a persistent hub you build and grow over time. Your equipped chicken and pet are visible here, and the whole hub comes alive as you upgrade it.

### 🏗️ Hub Upgrades & Buildings
Spend seeds to upgrade three structures in your hub:
- **Seed Silo** — Generates passive seeds over time while you're away. Collected automatically on launch.
- **Training Nest** — Boosts XP earned per level cleared (+20% per upgrade level). Also speeds up egg incubation.
- **Playground** — Grows with the Sanctuary as development continues.

### 🥚 Hatchery & Egg System
Eggs now drop randomly after completing levels, with three rarity tiers:
- 🟤 **Brown Egg** — Common
- 🔵 **Blue Egg** — Uncommon
- 🥇 **Golden Egg** — Rare (higher chance at deeper levels)

Eggs incubate in real time. Upgrade the Training Nest to hatch faster. A notification dot on the Hub button tells you when eggs are ready.

### ⭐ Player XP & Leveling
You now have a persistent **player level** and XP bar. Earn XP by clearing levels and finishing runs. Each level-up rewards **Feather Points** — the currency of the Skill Tree.

### 🌿 Skill Tree (21 Skills, 4 Paths)
Unlock permanent passive abilities using Feather Points across four skill paths:

| Path | Skills |
|---|---|
| 👁️ **Scout** | Quick Eyes, Wider Vision, Hazard Sense |
| 🛡️ **Tank** | Shield Master, Tough Feathers, Blast Resistance |
| 🔍 **Merchant** | Seed Finder, Bargain Hunter, Golden Touch |
| 🐔 **Skin** | Unique 3-skill tree for each skin you own |

Each path follows **Basic → Rare → Epic** — you must unlock the previous tier before advancing. Skin skills are tied to skin ownership, giving real value to collecting every skin.

#### Skin-Exclusive Skills
| Skin | Basic | Rare | Epic |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🚀 Space | Zero Gravity (15% mine skip) | Orbit Scan (+2 reveal tiles) | Warp Step (teleport once per run) |
| 🥷 Ninja | Shadow Step (first tile always safe) | Smoke Bomb (free peek every 3) | Assassin's Eye (mines show faint shadow) |
| 👑 Royal | Royal Decree (+15% seeds) | Tax Collection (bonus cache every 5 levels) | Golden Crown (20% shop discount) |
| 👻 Ghost | Phase Through (20% mine pass) | Haunting (auto-reveal nearby mines) | Possession (survive one death per run) |

### 🔁 Endless Mode
A brand new game mode — an auto-scrolling tile runner across **5 themed worlds**:

| Theme | Hazard |
|---|---|
| 🐔 Classic | None |
| 🔥 Lava | Heat waves |
| ❄️ Ice | Slippery tiles |
| 🌿 Jungle | Overgrowth |
| 🌌 Galaxy | Gravity anomalies |

The grid scrolls upward continuously. Tap tiles to survive. Difficulty and scroll speed ramp up the longer you last. Theme changes every few floors.

### 🌍 5 Biomes
The game world is now built around five distinct environments, each with unique hazards and tile styles: **Green Meadows, Glacial Tundra, Volcanic Forge, Midnight Swamp,** and **Windswept Plains**.

- 📐 **Scaling grid** — grows from 6×6 to 10×9 as levels increase
- 💣 **Mine density scaling** — 20% at level 1, up to 60% at level 20+

- 🌾 **Seed economy** — earn, spend, and invest in your run and your hub

- 🐾 **5 pets** — each with a unique passive ability

- 📅 **Daily Challenge** — one seeded level per day, one attempt
